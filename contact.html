My name is Zoe Greenwald. I've been shooting for about 8 years, and gotten much more serious in the last three. I mainly photograph birds, but the occasional landscape, or scene will catch my eye. My current equipment is a Nikon z7ii with a 200-500mm zoom lense. I'm based in Burlington, VT and have been fortunate enough to travel to some incredible places like Japan and Iceland where I had the opportunity to photograph birds not local to New England.
